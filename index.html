About Us

A Family Approach to Estate Sales

Legacy Estate Sales was founded on a simple belief: what you’ve built deserves to be represented with dignity, expertise, and care. An estate sale is more than just a transaction — it’s a meaningful transition.

As a family-owned and operated business, we approach every home with compassion, patience, and respect. Whether you’re downsizing, managing a loved one’s estate, or moving into a new chapter, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

We believe your belongings carry history, memories, and significance — and they deserve to be handled with care.

Our Philosophy

Where Care and Expertise Meet.

  • Unmatched Trust & Discretion

    We handle every estate with confidentiality, professionalism, and integrity.

  • Family-Centered Service

    We understand the emotional side of estate sales and work closely with families to make the process as smooth as possible.

  • Luxury-Level Presentation

    Every item is thoughtfully staged, photographed, and marketed to attract discerning buyers.

  • Knowledge You Can Rely On

    With deep knowledge of antiques and market demand, we ensure your estate is priced and positioned correctly.
